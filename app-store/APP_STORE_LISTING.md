## Tips for Approval

### Apple App Store
1. **Be honest** - Don't claim features you don't have
2. **Test thoroughly** - Reviewers WILL find bugs
3. **Explain permissions** - Why you need location, camera, etc.
4. **Provide demo account** - Make testing easy for reviewers
5. **Be patient** - First review takes 24 hours to 7 days
6. **Expect rejection** - 30-40% of first submissions are rejected

### Google Play Store
1. **Complete data safety** - Be transparent about data collection
2. **Content rating** - Answer IARC questionnaire honestly
3. **Target API level** - Ensure you meet minimum requirements
4. **Test on Android 13+** - Most users are on recent versions
5. **Faster reviews** - Usually approved in 1-3 days
