## Testing Requirements

Before finalizing, please ensure:

1. **Test at 60x60px** - This is the actual size on a phone home screen
2. **Test on different backgrounds:**
   - White background
   - Black background
   - Colorful wallpapers
   - Gradient backgrounds
3. **Test in context:**
   - Among other app icons (Instagram, TikTok, etc.)
   - In App Store search results
   - On notification tray
4. **Monochrome test:**
   - Convert to grayscale - should still be recognizable
5. **Quick recognition test:**
   - Show to someone for 2 seconds - can they describe it?
